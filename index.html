Social Science Dashboard Inator

Social science should be built on persistent, consistent measurement. Data collection and analysis should be transparent processes. The Social Science Dashboard Inator integrates these principles, with the added benefits of standardization and automation.

Persistent

Continuous measurement instead of one-off studies.

Consistent

A single operationalization with repeated measurement.

Transparent

Shared microdata, downloadable analysis scripts.

Standardized

One file, standard column structure.

Automated

Easy setup. New data arrives in your email inbox every day. Analysis scripts re-run while you sleep.
